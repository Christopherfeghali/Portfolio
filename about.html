Image analysis/segmentation tool that that takes in images as input. In addition to the uploaded image, a target area (Rectangle) or an indication of the object that needs segmentation is drawn using interaction from the user. After the target has been placed on the uploaded image that specific area will be segmented/partitioned. After the processing is completed an output of the segmented image is given where the foreground is separated from the background thus outlining the object/human shape in the image. The results of the implemented segmentation (Grab-cut) is then compared with results from other methods of image segmentation
